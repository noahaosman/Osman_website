Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion
Statement

For the majority of my life a passion for science and mathematics have been a defining part of my identity and the basis of how I understand the world. Over the past few years, it has become increasingly obvious that the opportunities necessary for me to explore and develop this passion were granted as a direct result of the privileges that I am fortunate enough to hold. It is my belief that the disparate access to STEM across racial and social classes in this country is antithetical to societal advancement as a whole, and the expansion of access to STEM is one of the most important issues facing the field today.
